LayoutRenderer - Slot Creation Demo

Instructions

  • Floating Add Button: Click the blue + button in the top-left to add a slot anywhere
  • Slot Menus: Click the ••• button on existing slots for options
  • Insertion Points: Hover over containers to see insertion points appear
  • Add Slot Here: Click on the dashed blue lines to add slots at specific positions
  • Slot Configuration: Fill out the form to configure your new slot
